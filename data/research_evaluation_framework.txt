Research Quality Evaluation Framework

1. Problem Statement
- Is the research question clearly defined?
- Is it specific and measurable?
- Does it address a real gap in literature?

2. Literature Review
- Are relevant papers cited?
- Is the review recent (last 5–10 years)?
- Does it critically analyze prior work?

3. Methodology
- Is the method appropriate for the research question?
- Is sample size sufficient?
- Are assumptions clearly stated?
- Are baselines compared?

4. Data Quality
- Is dataset publicly available?
- Is preprocessing explained?
- Is data biased?

5. Experimental Design
- Are experiments reproducible?
- Are hyperparameters disclosed?
- Are train/test splits proper?

6. Statistical Validity
- Are statistical tests used?
- Are p-values or confidence intervals reported?
- Is overfitting addressed?

7. Results & Analysis
- Are results compared to SOTA?
- Are ablation studies performed?
- Are limitations discussed?

8. Ethical Considerations
- Bias?
- Privacy?
- Environmental impact?

9. Reproducibility Score Factors
- Code available?
- Dataset available?
- Random seed mentioned?
